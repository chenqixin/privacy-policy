Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025 | Effective: January 1, 2025

Welcome to PANADA Smart Robot Control App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy and personal information security very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide robot control services, we may collect the following information:

  • Bluetooth Device Information - Used to scan and connect to your robot devices, including device name and MAC address
  • Camera Access - Used to scan device QR codes for quick pairing
  • Location Information - Required by the Android system for Bluetooth scanning functionality
  • Device Identifiers - Used to identify and manage your paired robot devices

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing Bluetooth connection and control functionality for robot devices
  • Saving your device pairing records for quick reconnection
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Troubleshooting and fixing technical issues

3. Information Storage & Protection

  • Your device pairing information is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your sensitive personal information
  • This app does not include any user account system and requires no registration or login
  • We employ reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access

4. Information Sharing

We commit to:

  • Never selling your personal information to any third party
  • Never sharing your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent
  • Never using your information for advertising or commercial marketing

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • You can disable Bluetooth, Camera, and Location permissions at any time in system settings
  • You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • You can contact us at any time to inquire about how your personal information is processed

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 14 years of age. If you are a guardian and discover that your child has used this app without your consent, please contact us and we will handle it promptly.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page, and we will notify you within the app for significant changes. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
